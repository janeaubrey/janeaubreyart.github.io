Please get in touch to arrange a visit. We are happy to welcome collectors and those with a serious interest in the work.

Getting here

  • By train: Redruth station is on the main Penzance to London Paddington line. Mawla is a short taxi ride from the station.
  • By car: Leave the A30 at the Redruth junction and follow signs towards Redruth. Mawla is a small village just north of the town.
  • Parking: Available at the property — please mention when arranging your visit.
