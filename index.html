About the artist

Jane Doe is a painter and photographer working from her studio in Penzance, Cornwall. Her practice is rooted in the landscape of the far west — the moors, the cliffs, and the ever-present sea.

Drawing on a background in fine art and years spent observing the Atlantic light, Jane creates works that balance observation with feeling. Each piece begins outdoors, often in difficult conditions, before being developed in the studio.

Her paintings are held in private collections across the UK and internationally. She exhibits regularly at galleries in Cornwall and beyond, and welcomes visitors to her studio by appointment.
